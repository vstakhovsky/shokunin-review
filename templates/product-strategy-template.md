# Product Strategy Template

Template for Product Strategy documents.

## Structure

```markdown
# [Strategy Name] Product Strategy

## Strategic Thesis

**Thesis**:
[What is the core strategic choice?]

**Strategic Choice**:
[What are we choosing?]

**Why This Matters**:
[Why is this important?]

---

## Target Customer / ICP

**Primary Segment**:
- Description: [Who are they?]
- Size: [how many?]
- Characteristics: [key traits]

**Ideal Customer Profile**:
- Demographics: [age, location, etc.]
- Psychographics: [behaviors, preferences]
- Pain Points: [what problems do they have]

**Not Targeting**:
- [Who we're not focusing on]
- [Why not?]

---

## Pain Points

**Primary Pain**:
[What is the main pain point?]

**Evidence**:
- Customer quotes: [examples]
- Data: [supporting numbers]
- Frequency: [how often?]
- Impact: [quantified if possible]

**Secondary Pains**:
- [Pain 2]: [description]
- [Pain 3]: [description]

---

## Evidence

**Market Evidence**:
- Market size: [TAM]
- Growth rate: [YoY growth]
- Trend: [market direction]

**Customer Evidence**:
- Survey data: [key findings]
- Interview insights: [key quotes]
- Churn analysis: [data]

**Competitive Evidence**:
- Competitor A: [what they do]
- Competitor B: [what they do]
- Gap: [opportunity]

**Internal Evidence**:
- A/B test results: [findings]
- Operations data: [insights]
- Finance data: [costs, revenue]

---

## Opportunity Sizing

**Market Size (TAM)**:
- [Total addressable market]
- Growth rate: [YoY %]
- Source: [how calculated]

**Serviceable Market (SAM)**:
- [Our target markets]
- Current penetration: [%]
- Growth rate: [YoY %]

**Target Segment (SOM)**:
- [Our specific focus]
- Current users: [number]
- Target users: [number]
- Time to reach: [duration]

**Revenue Potential**:
- Current ARPU: [$]
- Potential ARPU: [$]
- Revenue opportunity: [calculation]
- Gross margin: [%]
- Gross profit: [calculation]

---

## Business Logic

**Value Creation**:
[How does this create value?]

**Economic Logic**:
[Unit economics, payback period]

**Competitive Advantage**:
- [What's defensible?]
- [Why can't competitors copy this easily?]

---

## Options Considered

**Option 1**: [description]
- Pros: [advantages]
- Cons: [disadvantages]
- Revenue: [potential]
- Rejected: [why]

**Option 2**: [description]
- Pros: [advantages]
- Cons: [disadvantages]
- Revenue: [potential]
- Rejected: [why]

**Chosen Approach**: [Option 3]
- Why: [rationale]
- Revenue: [potential]
- Risks: [what could go wrong]

---

## Trade-offs

**Trade-off 1**: [what we're accepting]
- Rationale: [why]
- Mitigation: [how we'll mitigate]

**Trade-off 2**: [what we're accepting]
- Rationale: [why]
- Mitigation: [how we'll mitigate]

---

## Resource Requirements

**Team Resources**:
- [Role 1]: [count, duration]
- [Role 2]: [count, duration]

**Budget Requirements**:
- Development: [$]
- Infrastructure: [$/year]
- Total first-year: [$]

**Timeline Estimate**:
- Phase 1 (MVP): [duration]
- Phase 2 (Scale): [duration]
- Phase 3 (Optimize): [duration]
- Total: [duration to full capability]

---

## Risks

**Strategic Risk**:
- Risk: [what could go wrong?]
- Probability: [High/Medium/Low]
- Impact: [High/Medium/Low]
- Mitigation: [how we'll address]

**Execution Risk**:
- Risk: [what could go wrong?]
- Probability: [High/Medium/Low]
- Impact: [High/Medium/Low]
- Mitigation: [how we'll address]

**Market Risk**:
- Risk: [what could go wrong?]
- Probability: [High/Medium/Low]
- Impact: [High/Medium/Low]
- Mitigation: [how we'll address]

---

## Sequencing / Phasing

**Phase 1**: [MVP - 3 months]
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- Success: [criteria]

**Phase 2**: [Growth - 6 months]
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- Success: [criteria]

**Phase 3**: [Expansion - 12 months]
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- Success: [criteria]

**Dependencies**:
- Phase 2 depends on: [Phase 1 success]
- Phase 3 depends on: [Phase 2 success]

---

## GTM / Adoption Strategy

**Launch Strategy**:
- [Phase 1]: [duration, exposure]
- [Phase 2]: [duration, exposure]
- [Phase 3]: [duration, exposure]

**Adoption Drivers**:
- [Driver 1]: [how we'll drive adoption]
- [Driver 2]: [how we'll drive adoption]

**Success Metrics**:
- Adoption: [target %]
- Engagement: [target metric]
- Retention: [target metric]

---

## Success Metrics

**Leading Indicators**:
- [Metric 1]: [target, by when]
- [Metric 2]: [target, by when]

**Lagging Indicators**:
- [Metric 1]: [target, by when]
- [Metric 2]: [target, by when]

**Targets by Phase**:
- Phase 1: [metric targets]
- Phase 2: [metric targets]
- Phase 3: [metric targets]

**Business Impact**:
- ROI: [calculation]
- Payback: [timeline]
- NPV: [calculation]

---

## Decision Ask

**Request**:
[What strategic decision needed?]

**Decision Maker**:
- [Role 1]
- [Role 2]

**Timeline**:
[When decision needed?]

**Commitment Required**:
- Budget: [$ amount]
- Resources: [team, time]
- Strategic focus: [what we're committing to]

**Decision Options**:
1. Approve strategy (recommended)
2. Approve with modifications
3. Reject / Defer (with rationale)
```
