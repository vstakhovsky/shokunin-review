# A/B Test: Free Shipping Threshold

## Overview
Test whether offering free shipping on orders over $50 increases average order value.

## Hypothesis
Customers will add more items to their cart to qualify for free shipping, increasing average order value.

## Success Metrics
- Increase average order value
- Improve conversion rate

## Experiment Design
- Show free shipping offer to treatment group
- Current shipping costs apply to control group
- Run for 4 weeks

## Target
All customers who visit checkout page

## Timeline
Launch immediately, run for 4 weeks
