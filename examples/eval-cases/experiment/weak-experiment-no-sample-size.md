# A/B Test: Recommendation Widget Placement

## Overview
Test if moving the recommendation widget higher on the product page increases click-through rate.

## Hypothesis
Placing recommendations near the top of the page will increase engagement compared to current placement near the bottom.

## Success Metrics
- Increase recommendation click-through rate
- Maintain or improve conversion rate

## Experiment Design
- Treatment: Widget at top of page, below add to cart
- Control: Current placement at bottom
- 50/50 split

## Expected Impact
- CTR increase from 2% to 3%
- Potential revenue increase

## Launch Plan
Deploy and monitor results
