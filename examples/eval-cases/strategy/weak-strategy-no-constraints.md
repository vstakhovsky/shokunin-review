# Mobile App Growth Strategy

## Objective
Achieve 5M monthly active users by end of 2025.

## Current State
- 2M monthly active users
- Growth rate of 10% month-over-month
- Strong retention in core markets

## Growth Drivers
- New user acquisition
- Improved retention
- Increased engagement
- Viral features

## Investment Priorities
- Product enhancements
- Marketing campaigns
- Partnership development
- International expansion

## Execution Plan
- Q1: Launch new features
- Q2: Expand to new markets
- Q3: Major marketing push
- Q4: Optimization and refinement
